PC Purifier

Local-only processing:

  • All cleanup, scanning, and optimization happens on your device.
  • No data is sent to our servers or third parties.
  • No telemetry, crash reports, or usage tracking.
  • No internet connection required for core features.

Privacy Dashboard feature:

  • Shows you which apps have camera, microphone, location, and file access.

  • Gives you shortcuts to Windows settings to revoke permissions.

License validation:

  • Pro license keys are validated locally using cryptographic signatures.
  • No phone-home validation or online activation required.

Ship Studio

Local-first design:
