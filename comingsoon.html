New product idea

Browser extension: “privacy/legal translator”

Live-reads consent text and policy wording, then displays a plain-English breakdown of what you’re agreeing to. The goal is clarity — not fear — and a fast way to spot mismatches (example: a simple flashlight app asking for contacts).
