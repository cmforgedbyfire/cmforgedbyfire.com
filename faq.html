General Questions

PC Purifier

Is PC Purifier free?

Yes. The core version is completely free and includes Quick Clean, Deep Clean, Health monitoring, Startup Manager, Shield shortcuts, and the Privacy Dashboard. The Pro version ($7.99 one-time) adds game requirements checking, audit reports, and advanced cleanup targets.

What's included in PC Purifier Pro?

Pro features include:

  • Game requirements checker (can my PC run this game?)
  • Audit reports (exportable cleanup and health logs)
  • Advanced cleanup targets (additional junk categories)
  • Priority email support

Pro is a one-time $7.99 purchase. No subscriptions.

How do I get my Pro license?

After purchasing through PayPal, you'll receive your license key via email within 24 hours. Copy the key and paste it into PC Purifier under Settings → Activate Pro.

What does PC Purifier clean?

PC Purifier removes temporary files, browser cache, Windows update leftovers, recycle bin contents, thumbnail cache, error logs, and other safe-to-delete junk. The Deep Clean adds Windows.old folders, delivery optimization files, and system restore cleanup.

Is PC Purifier safe to use?

Yes. PC Purifier only targets known-safe temporary and cache files. We don't delete system files, user documents, or program data. You can review what will be cleaned before running any operation.

Does PC Purifier send my data anywhere?

No. PC Purifier has zero telemetry. All scans, cleanups, and reports stay on your computer. We don't collect usage statistics or diagnostic data.

Ship Studio

What is Ship Studio?

Ship Studio is a local-first toolkit for indie developers to package, document, and ship software projects. It automates release workflows, generates changelogs, and helps turn messy projects into polished releases.

Does Ship Studio require internet?

No. Ship Studio is local-first and works completely offline. All your projects and data stay on your machine.

What platforms does Ship Studio support?

Ship Studio runs on Windows. Support for macOS and Linux is planned for future releases.
