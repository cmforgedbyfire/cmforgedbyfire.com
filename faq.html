Why does Windows say a download is not commonly downloaded?

Microsoft Defender SmartScreen can warn about new downloads until enough people have downloaded and trusted them. This can happen with any new Windows app on the site. If you started from cmforgedbyfire.com or the official cmforgedbyfire GitHub release, open the downloads menu, choose Keep, then choose Keep anyway.
