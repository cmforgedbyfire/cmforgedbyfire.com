# Forged By Fire Website Brand Guide

## Core Signal

Forged By Fire should feel practical, direct, private, and built by hand. The website should show real tools, real screenshots, and clear next steps before marketing copy.

## Visual Direction

- Use the forge palette already in `css/style.css`: ember, brass, teal, steel, dark stone, and warm panels.
- Use product icons and screenshots as primary visual assets.
- Keep OTTO on `ai.html`; do not turn him into a separate product site until the public chat is mature.
- Keep page sections simple: hero, useful context, product cards, proof, privacy, download or chat action.
- Avoid decorative-only gradients, oversized marketing blocks, and generic stock imagery.

## Voice

- Plain English.
- No hype.
- Be clear about what is local, what is public, and what may be logged.
- Say what a tool does, who it helps, and what state it is in.

## OTTO Placement

- OTTO belongs on the AI page as the first-viewport product.
- Website OTTO is a public preview connected to a public-safe knowledge base.
- Desktop OTTO is the harder, more powerful track: OCR, local files, memory, and trusted local workflows.
- Never imply the public website chat can access private files, private memory, computer control, or OCR uploads.
